About Music Mood

Music Mood uses a song's meta data to classify it into 3 broad moods

Hundreds of songs from the respective moods were selected and used for training for this bot

Concept Of Music Mood

Training Data: Metadata from hundreds of songs
Meta Data: Audio features like tempo, instrumentalness etc.

ML Algorithm: It is a Logistic Regression model

Collecting Data: Spotify API allows a call to get audio features

Genre Wise Variation: Different genres have different patterns of audio features for the same mood
Handling Different Genres: Multiple models were trained, one per genre

Accuracy: An average of 90% accuracy was achieved

When people with similar music tastes were given the same samples , most of their answers matched the ML output


The other answers were in sync with the confusion matrix
