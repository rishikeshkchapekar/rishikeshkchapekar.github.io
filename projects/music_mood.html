Music Mood

AI bot to classify songs into different moods

About Music Mood

About Music Mood

Music Mood was created as a part of a larger project, which is still under development

It uses a song's meta data to classify it into 3 broad moods

Hundreds of songs from the respective moods were selected and used for training for this bot

Concept Of Music Mood

Music Mood used various meta data of the songs to generate the ML model. Meta data included features like tempo,danceability,instrumentalness etc.

It is a Logistic Regression model

Thanks to Spotify API,it was an easy task getting the audio features for the song. However, problem was that the songs needed to be of the same genre to get a satisfactory output.

The reason for this is that due to the varying style of playing music in different genres, the audio features for the same mood would vastly vary.

As a result,multiple models were made, one for one genre respectively. The end result was highly satisfying with an accuracy as high as 90% for something as subjective as a taste in music

People with similar tastes were asked to classify the mood of the song, and almost all answers were in support of the AI model,the others being in sync with its confusion matrix
