This is a security software designed to raise an alarm when it detects someone drowning

About Drown Alarm

Drown alarm uses the live feed of a security camera watching over a swimming pool

Convolutional Neural Networks are used to analyse a sequence of frames, detect a person in it, and determine whether he/she is drowning

How It Works

Input: Live video feed from security camera
Images: Takes a sequence of images for analysis.

ML Algorithm: Convolutional Neural Networks are used for classification
Data: Large dataset of image sequences of people drowing against people not downing

Why sequence of images: To be able to differentiate between actual drowning and false positive cases
False Positive Cases: Swimming underwater,diving in the pool etc.

The images shown here are not from the actual CCTV footage (privacy issues)
