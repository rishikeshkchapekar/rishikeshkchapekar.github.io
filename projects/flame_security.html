About Fire Alarm AI

About Fire Alarm AI

This was a fire alarm taking input from a security camera installed in a room, passing the image through a Convolutional Neural Network to detect a fire

In this system, ambient sensors were also added as a source of cofirmation.

In case of fire, alarms were raised and the sensor readings as well as the security camera image were uploaded to a database

How It Works

The security system has two input points: The live video feed from a security camera, and ambient sensors such as temperature and humidity. The sensors were added to increase the accuracy of the fire detection

The final results, i.e if a fire was confirmed, were uploaded to a database with timestamps for keeping records. An alarm was also raised to alert the people in the building

The system was meant to detect small indoor fires and alert people in charge immediately to stop the fire from spreading any further

The whole system was running on a Raspberry Pi,making it very cost effective and highly efficient
