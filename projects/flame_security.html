About Fire Alarm AI

About Fire Alarm AI

This was a fire alarm taking input from a security camera installed in a room, passing the image through a Convolutional Neural Network to detect a fire

In this system, ambient sensors were also added as a source of cofirmation.

In case of fire, alarms were raised and the sensor readings as well as the security camera image were uploaded to a database

How It Works

Input: Two input points - live security camera video feed and ambient sensors
Ambient Sensors: For increasing accuracy of true positive, temperature sensor and humidity sensors were used

Detecting Fire: Convolutional Neural Networks were used to detect fire in the image

Keeping Records: Positive results were uploaded to a database with sensor readings and image

Host: Hosted on a Raspberry Pi, making it very cost effective
