About AI-Music

About AI Music

AI-Music started in 2020 initially as a way for me to generate various guitar music pieces. It uses the concept of Recurrent Neural Networks to understand the flow of musical notes and subsequently create a new one.

The project faced many challenges initially such as making the output files sound like a real instrument, incorporating various music playing techniques etc.

Eventually it did reach a point where the output generated was more than satisfactory

The Development Of AI-Music

Challenges Faced

There were many challenges faced during the initial development:

1-Collecting data: Songs from the same genre have highly varying sounds as it all depends on the artist's creativity

2-Arranging Data: Through the initial models, it became evident that the training data needed to be of songs that sounded similar to each other to generate satisfying results

3-Generating realistic instrument sounds:The project began with the intention to create guitar sounds, but making computer generated guitar sounds sound realistic was a tough task

4-Generating melodies with musical creativity:Playing notes with varying time intervals and still staying in beat was another challenge

Current Development Model

Training Data: Multiple models have been made each using different kinds of music such as western classical, pop music from the mid 70s etc.

Instruments: The output of the current model sounds best when played as a violin or an organ pipe

Musical accuracy: The music generated sounds like a proper song with a proper intro and outro and all the other sub sections of the song

Other Development Steps: The goal is to be able to create realistic music from as many instruments as possible, thus making it possible to generate an entire song
