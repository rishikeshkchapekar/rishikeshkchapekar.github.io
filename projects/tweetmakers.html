TweetMakers

TweetMakers is an AI bot designed to generate tweets in the style of certain Twitter users, as well as on certain topics

Go To TweetMakers

About TweetMakers

The TweetMakers bot uses the concept of Recurrent Neural Networks to generate tweets

Capable of generating tweets in the style of certain Twitter users and topics. It is meant for the sole purpose of entertainment

The site also has a share option to allow users to share tweets directly to Twitter

Understanding TweetMakers©

  • The site provides multiple options to choose from
  • A tweet is generated in the style of the persona the user selects


  • Concept of Recurrent Neural Networks is used to predict a sequence of words from an initial seed
  • Seed is auto generated based on the persona selected


  • Tweet generated is always in context of the selected persona


  • A share option is also provided which lets the user share the generated tweet directly to Twitter
